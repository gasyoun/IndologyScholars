Data Sources

The archive distinguishes between primary corpus materials representing the actual conference records and external authority databases used for context and entity disambiguation.

Primary Corpus Sources

Zograf Readings
Programs and lists of presentations from the St. Petersburg Readings (2004–present). Cached files are stored in html_cache/zograf_*.html.
Roerich Readings
Programs and lists of presentations from the Moscow Readings (2007–present). Cached files are stored in html_cache/roerich_*.html.
Curated Seed Data
Explicit event dates, session chairs, venue configurations, and media mappings are maintained in zograf-roerich-db.md.

External Authority Mappings

To link our local corpus with the global scientific web, we match scholars and institutions to external authority files. These external records are treated as verified assertions, not primary data sources:

Authority Overrides
Canonical identifiers, latin names, and manual overrides are loaded from authority_ids.json.
Scholarly & Citation Databases
Optional connections to ORCID, Wikidata, VIAF, OpenAlex, Google Scholar, and РИНЦ/eLIBRARY are recorded when manually verified.
Institutional Registries
Normalized organizations are linked to ROR (Research Organization Registry) to ensure global interoperability.
